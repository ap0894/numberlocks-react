## About NumberLocks

NumberLocks is a unique puzzle game that combines strategy and math in a beautifully designed experience. Swipe tiles, subtract numbers, and clear the board to solve each challenging puzzle.

### How to Play

1. **Swipe** a numbered tile toward another tile
2. **Subtract** the smaller number from the larger
3. **Clear** all tiles to complete the level
4. **Earn keys** based on your efficiency

It's simple to learn but challenging to master!
