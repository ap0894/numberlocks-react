## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I play NumberLocks?

NumberLocks is a number puzzle game where you swipe tiles to subtract numbers and clear the board.

**Basic Rules:**
- Swipe a tile with a number toward another tile
- The numbers subtract: larger number - smaller number
- The result stays on the board
- Clear all tiles to complete the level
- Earn keys based on how efficiently you solve each puzzle

### How do I unlock new vaults?

Progress through the game by earning keys:
- **Vault 1 (Subtract)**: Available from the start
- **Vault 2 (Diagonal)**: Requires 21 keys, unlocks diagonal swipes
- **Vault 3 (4x4)**: Requires 50 keys, introduces 4x4 grids

### Can I reset my progress?

Yes! You can reset your progress by:
1. Going to Settings (gear icon)
2. Deleting the app and reinstalling it

### The game is frozen or not responding

Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
4. If issues persist, contact support

### How do I turn off sound or haptics?

1. Tap the gear icon (Settings) from the home screen
2. Toggle Sound Effects or Haptics on/off

## Troubleshooting

### Game won't launch
1. Restart your device
2. Check for app updates in the App Store
3. Ensure you have iOS 15.0 or later
4. Delete and reinstall the app

### Progress not saving
1. Make sure you're not in Low Power Mode
2. Check device storage (ensure you have space)
3. Contact support if issue persists

### Can't unlock next vault
Make sure you've earned enough keys:
- Vault 2: 21 keys required
- Vault 3: 50 keys required

You can see your total keys on the home screen.
